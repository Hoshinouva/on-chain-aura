# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Bored Web3 natives and crypto users. Context is casual, fun engagement—looking for a quick, entertaining readout of their on-chain identity based on wallet history.

## Product Purpose
Provide a mystical "Aura" reading based on a user's wallet address. Goal is fun engagement, shareability, and entertaining the Web3 community.

## Brand Personality
Fun, mystical, Web3-native.

## Anti-references
- SaaS generic (no corporate cream/blue, no sterile dashboards, no standard "hero-metric" boilerplate).

## Design Principles
- **Mystical over Technical:** Hide the blockchain math; surface the magic.
- **Engage Quickly:** Get to the "reveal" with minimal friction.
- **Web3 Native but Polished:** Lean into crypto culture without looking unrefined.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Standard contrast where possible, but visual aesthetic (mystical/dark) drives primary decisions. Ensure screen readers can parse the result.
